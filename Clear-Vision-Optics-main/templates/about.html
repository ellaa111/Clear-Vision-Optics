Who We Are

Clear Vision Optics was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to bring world-class eye care and premium eyewear to the people of Migori County. What started as a small shop in Kehancha Town has grown into the region's most trusted optical destination.

We believe that everyone deserves clear vision. Whether you're a student struggling to see the blackboard, a professional needing computer glasses, or a parent concerned about your child's eye health, we're here to help.

Our team of certified opticians combines professional expertise with personalized care. We don't just sell glasses—we provide comprehensive vision solutions tailored to your lifestyle, budget, and unique visual needs.

Prescription Glasses
